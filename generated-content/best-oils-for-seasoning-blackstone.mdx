## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Oils For Seasoning Blackstone

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you choose an oil, consider the material of your griddle and how often you use it. While most oils are universal, some are better suited for initial seasoning on a brand-new Blackstone, while others excel at daily maintenance. If you are starting from scratch, a wax-based rub or a dedicated conditioner like the official Blackstone kit might provide a thicker initial barrier. For daily users, a light liquid spray or a refined coconut oil can keep the surface slick without building up too much heavy residue.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
You can find seasoning oils in a range from $10 to $30. Basic vegetable or canola oils are the cheapest but may go rancid or smoke excessively. Specialized blends featuring avocado or flaxseed oil usually sit in the $15-$25 range.
