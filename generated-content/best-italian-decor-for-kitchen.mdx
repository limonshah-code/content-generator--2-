## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Italian Decor For Kitchen

### Find Your Need
Before you start shopping, consider whether you want purely decorative items or functional decor. If you have a small kitchen, items like the ravioli spoon rest or chef towels are excellent because they serve a purpose while looking great. If you have plenty of wall space, canvas sets or metal signs are the way to go to fill those empty gaps and create a mood.

### Budget
Set a realistic budget for your kitchen transformation. Small accents like magnets or signs typically range from $10 to $20. More substantial pieces like premium trivets or large figurines can go up to $50.
