## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Built In Wall Microwave

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and Use Case
Before you purchase, you must decide between a standard wall microwave, a microwave drawer, or a combination oven. A wall microwave is typically installed at eye level and is great for reheating. A drawer microwave is installed under the counter, making it safer for lifting heavy items. If you lack space for two appliances, a combination oven like the GE Profile might be the best route.

### 2. Budget - Setting Realistic Price Expectations
Built-in microwaves are generally more expensive than countertop models. You can expect to spend anywhere from $400 for a compact unit to $3,000 for a luxury combination wall oven. Don't forget to budget for the trim kit, which can cost an additional $150 to $300, and professional installation, which is often required for hardwired units.

### 3. Key Features - Technical Specifications
Wattage is a major factor; aim for at least 1000 watts for consistent performance. Sensor cooking is another must-have, as it prevents overcooking by measuring moisture. If you enjoy baking or crisping food, look for models with convection or air fry capabilities. These features turn your microwave into a mini-oven.

### 4. Quality & Durability - Construction Matters
Look for stainless steel interiors, as they are more durable and easier to clean than plastic or painted ones. Check the hinge quality on drop-down or side-swing doors. Units like the KoolMore feature reinforced hinges that can support weight, which is a sign of long-term durability.

### 5. Brand Reputation - Manufacturer Trust
Brands like Frigidaire, GE, and Summit have long histories in the appliance industry. Choosing an established brand ensures that you will have access to replacement parts and warranty service if something goes wrong. Always check user reviews for long-term reliability reports.

### 6. Portability/Size - Space and Storage
Measure your cabinet cutout at least three times. Standard sizes are 24, 27, and 30 inches. If you have a 24-inch cabinet, you cannot fit a 30-inch unit without significant carpentry. Also, consider the depth of your cabinets, as built-in units require specific clearance for ventilation and wiring.

## Is it A Wise Decision to Buy built in wall microwave?

Investing in the Best Built In Wall Microwave is a highly wise decision for homeowners looking to increase their property value and kitchen efficiency. Unlike countertop models that clutter your workspace, built-in units are designed to integrate into your cabinetry, creating a seamless and high-end look. This aesthetic improvement is often a key selling point for modern home buyers.

Furthermore, the technology in built-in models is typically superior to budget countertop units. You get features like convection cooking, air frying, and advanced sensor technology that simply isn't available in cheaper models. While the initial cost is higher, the long-term benefits of a more organized kitchen and a more versatile cooking environment outweigh the price. It is an investment in both the beauty and the functionality of your home.

## What Is the Best Way to Choose built in wall microwave?

The best way to choose your new microwave is to follow a systematic approach. First, measure your existing cabinet cutout or plan your new layout with a professional. This will dictate the size you can buy. Next, determine your primary use case. If you mostly reheat coffee, a basic model is fine. If you want to bake chickens or air fry fries, you need a convection combo.

Avoid the common mistake of ignoring the trim kit compatibility. Many people buy a microwave only to find the matching trim kit is out of stock or discontinued. Buy them together if possible. Also, consider the height of the users in your house. If you have children or shorter adults, a microwave drawer is much safer and more accessible than a high wall-mounted unit. Finally, check the power requirements; some high-end convection models require a 20-amp circuit rather than a standard 15-amp one.

### What is the best built in wall microwave for beginners?
A standard Frigidaire or Summit model with simple quick-start buttons is best for beginners. Look for units with one-touch presets for common foods like popcorn and potatoes. These models offer reliable performance without the complexity of advanced convection settings.

### How much should I spend on built in wall microwave?
For a quality built-in unit, expect to spend between $500 and $1,200. This range covers most high-performance models with sensor cooking. If you want a combination oven or a luxury drawer, the price can climb to $2,500.

### What features are most important in built in wall microwave?
Sensor cooking is the most important feature for daily use. It ensures your food is heated perfectly every time. Following that, high wattage (1000W+) and an easy-to-clean stainless steel interior are critical for performance and maintenance.

### How do I maintain my built in wall microwave?
Wipe the interior with a damp cloth and mild soap after use to prevent grease buildup. For stainless steel exteriors, use a dedicated cleaner to remove fingerprints. Ensure the vents remain clear of dust to prevent overheating.

### Are expensive built in wall microwave worth it?
Yes, if you use your microwave for more than just reheating. Expensive models offer convection baking, air frying, and better safety features like cool-touch glass. They also typically have a longer lifespan and better warranty support.

### What brands make the best built in wall microwave?
GE, Frigidaire, and Summit are top-tier brands. GE and Frigidaire are known for their advanced technology and sleek designs, while Summit excels in providing specialized sizes and powerful industrial-grade motors.

### How long do built in wall microwave typically last?
A high-quality built-in microwave should last between 9 and 12 years with proper maintenance. Higher-end models from reputable brands often have better build quality, extending their lifespan towards the upper end of that range.

### Can I find good built in wall microwave on a budget?
Yes, models from brands like GASLAND offer great features like automatic drawers and silent modes at a more accessible price point. You can also save money by choosing a standard 24-inch unit over a large 30-inch convection combo.
